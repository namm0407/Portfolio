My interest began when I joined a program made by Microsoft in F2. They taught us how use to code to control LED lights. I found it fascinating to see how lines of code could bring lights to life, changing colors and patterns based on my commands.


This experience sparked my curiosity about how programming works and how it can be applied to solve real-world problems. It was a pivotal moment that influenced my decision to pursue a computer science major in university. The creativity and problem-solving aspects of programming continue to motivate me every day.
